Delete your Kadir AI account & data

App: Kadir AI (package ai.kadir.app)

You can delete your Kadir AI account and all associated data at any time, using either method below.

Option 1 — Request by email

Email sspandian.here@gmail.com from your registered phone’s contact, with the subject “Delete my Kadir AI account”, including the phone number you registered with. We will delete your account and data within 30 days and confirm by reply.

Option 2 — In the app

Open Kadir AI → Profile/Settings → Delete account (where available), and confirm. This permanently removes your account immediately.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (name, phone number, language, KYC status)
  • All your fields and their history (crop, soil, water, disease, yield)
  • Your scores (risk, credit), advisory/alerts, and income/expense ledger
  • Any marketplace listings/orders and forum posts you created

What may be retained

We may retain limited records where required by law (e.g., transaction/tax records) for the legally mandated period, and anonymized/aggregated data that can no longer identify you. Everything else is permanently removed.
