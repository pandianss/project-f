Kadir AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Kadir AI ("we", "us", "the app") is a farm-intelligence application for farmers in India. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. By using Kadir AI you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Kadir AI is operated by the Kadir AI team. For any privacy question or request, contact us at sspandian.here@gmail.com.

We do not store your raw Aadhaar number; identity references are tokenized.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide core features: digital farm twin, advisory and alerts, crop recommendations, risk and credit scores, disease prediction, market intelligence, and the marketplace.
  • To send you notifications you opt into (e.g., weather and disease-risk alerts).
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot and improve the app.
  • To comply with applicable law.

4. How we share your information

We share your farm data with institutions (such as banks, NBFCs, insurers, FPOs or government agencies) only when you grant explicit, scoped consent for a specific purpose. You can view and revoke these consents in the app. We also use service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, payment and logistics partners) who process data on our behalf under contract. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Data retention

Your farm history is retained to provide the longitudinal record that powers the app's intelligence, for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see Your rights).

6. Security

We protect data in transit using encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and apply access controls and audit logging to institutional access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Access — request a copy of your data.
  • Correction — ask us to fix inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data by emailing sspandian.here@gmail.com.
  • Consent control — grant or revoke sharing with institutions in the app.

8. Children

Kadir AI is intended for adults (18+) and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
