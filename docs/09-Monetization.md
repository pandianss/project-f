# FarmOS AI India — Monetization & Business Model

## 1. Strategy
Farmer-side = **acquisition + data flywheel** (low/zero ARPU). Institution-side = **revenue** (high willingness-to-pay, real budgets, real pain). Monetize the **accumulated farm intelligence**, not the weather app.

## 2. Revenue streams
| # | Stream | Buyer | Pricing model | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Farmer subscription (freemium → premium) | farmers | ₹0 free; ₹200–600/yr premium (ERP, advanced reco, priority) | thin; mainly retention |
| 2 | FPO subscription | FPOs | ₹/member/yr or platform fee | dashboards, bulk advisory |
| 3 | **Bank/NBFC/MFI** | lenders | **per-field-scored / per-API-call + SaaS seats** | core revenue |
| 4 | **Insurer** | insurers | per-policy risk price + per-claim assessment + CCE-replacement contracts | core revenue |
| 5 | Input company | seed/fert/pest cos | demand intelligence SaaS + **recommendation marketplace commission** | high margin |
| 6 | Government contracts | state/central | annual contracts, dashboards, scheme targeting | large, lumpy |
| 7 | API licensing | agritech/fintech | metered API | platform play |
| 8 | Data products | research/agri-business | aggregated, anonymized, consented reports | high margin, governance-sensitive |
| 9 | Carbon credit module (future) | farmers/buyers | % of credit value | regenerative-ag MRV |

## 3. Unit economics (illustrative)
- Cost to onboard + maintain a field/yr: declining from ~₹40 → ~₹10 at scale (compute + ingest amortized).
- **Credit score API:** ₹15–40 per field scored (batch) to lenders.
- **Insurance pricing/claim:** ₹20–80 per policy/claim.
- **Input marketplace:** 3–8% commission.
- The same field, once in the twin, is **monetized repeatedly** across lenders/insurers/input cos — marginal revenue per field rises with B2B coverage.

## 4. 3-year revenue projection (base case, ₹ Cr)
| | Y1 | Y2 | Y3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fields under mgmt | 0.5M | 5M | 20M |
| B2B credit/insurance | 1 | 18 | 85 |
| Input cos + marketplace | 0.5 | 8 | 30 |
| Govt contracts | 1 | 6 | 18 |
| Farmer/FPO subs | 0.2 | 2 | 7 |
| **Total ARR** | **~2.7** | **~34** | **~140** |

*Bull case ~₹250 Cr Y3 with national govt contract + multi-insurer scale; bear case ~₹70 Cr. Drivers: fields under management × B2B coverage per field × price.*

## 5. Why this compounds (moat)
Each field-season adds labeled outcomes (yield, repayment, claim). Models improve → scores get more accurate → lenders/insurers depend on them → switching cost rises → more fields onboarded via partners → more data. **Data network effect on the credit/insurance/risk triad.**
